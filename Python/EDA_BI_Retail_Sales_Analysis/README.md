# Retail Sales Analysis 

---

## Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to understand revenue patterns, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance. The objective is to explore historical sales data, identify trends, and extract insights that support data-driven business decisions.

The project demonstrates exploratory data analysis (EDA) and business-focused interpretation of retail data.

---

## Dataset
- Retail sales dataset
- Transaction-level sales records
- Numeric and categorical variables related to products, customers, and sales
- Structured tabular data for analysis

---

## Objectives
- Load and clean retail sales data
- Explore sales trends and distributions
- Analyze product and customer-level performance
- Identify key revenue drivers
- Summarize insights through clear visualizations

---

## Data Preparation
- Loaded retail data using Python
- Cleaned and validated sales-related fields
- Handled missing and inconsistent values
- Converted date fields for time-based analysis
- Prepared data for exploratory analysis

---

## Analysis & Modeling
- Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Analyzed sales distributions and trends over time
- Compared sales performance across products and categories
- Evaluated customer purchasing patterns
- Interpreted key business metrics

---

## Evaluation & Analysis
- Identified top-performing products and categories
- Observed seasonal and temporal sales trends
- Analyzed variability in sales volume and revenue
- Translated analytical findings into business insights

---
