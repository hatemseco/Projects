# Profitability & Cost Optimization Dashboard

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard focuses on analyzing profitability drivers across products, regions, and discount levels. The objective is to identify loss-making areas and opportunities for cost optimization and margin improvement.

## Key KPIs
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin (%)
- Discount Rate
- Loss-Making Products
- Profit by Region and Category

## Key Insights
- High discount levels are strongly associated with negative profit margins, indicating ineffective pricing strategies.
- A small subset of products contributes disproportionately to overall losses and should be reviewed or discontinued.
- Profitability varies significantly by region, highlighting opportunities for targeted regional pricing and cost control.
- Optimizing discount policies could significantly improve overall margins without reducing sales volume.
