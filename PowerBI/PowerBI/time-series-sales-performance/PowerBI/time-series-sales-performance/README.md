## Project Overview
This dashboard analyzes sales performance over time, highlighting revenue growth, profitability, and year-over-year (YoY) trends.

## Business Problem
How is the business performing year over year, and is growth being achieved while maintaining healthy profit margins?

## Key Insights
- Revenue shows consistent growth from 2016 to 2019, with the strongest acceleration in 2018.
- Year-over-year revenue growth peaked at ~29% in 2018.
- Growth moderated to ~20% in 2019, suggesting early signs of stabilization after rapid expansion.
- Profit margin remains healthy at ~12â€“13%, indicating growth was not achieved at the expense of profitability.
- Average order value (~459) suggests stable customer purchasing behavior alongside revenue growth.
