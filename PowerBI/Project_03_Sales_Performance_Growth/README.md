## Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales performance and growth trends over time. The objective is to evaluate revenue, profit, and order volume trends, and to identify periods of strong growth or decline.

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Year-over-Year Growth (%)

## Key Insights
- Sales show a consistent upward trend over the analyzed period, indicating healthy business growth.
- Revenue growth outpaced order growth, suggesting improvements in pricing strategy or average order value.
- Certain years experienced accelerated growth, highlighting the impact of seasonal demand and strategic initiatives.
- Profit trends generally follow revenue growth, confirming sustainable performance rather than volume-driven losses.
