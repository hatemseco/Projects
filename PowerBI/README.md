# Power BI Projects Portfolio

## Overview
This folder contains a collection of end-to-end Power BI projects focused on business analytics, KPI design, and data-driven storytelling. Each project addresses a real-world business problem and demonstrates the ability to transform raw data into actionable insights using interactive dashboards.

The projects emphasize clarity, performance tracking, and decision support for business stakeholders.

---

## Projects Included

### 1. E-Commerce Operations & Returns
Analyzes shipping efficiency, delivery performance, and product return risk to identify operational bottlenecks and improve service reliability.

**Key Focus:** Operations analytics, return rate analysis, service-level KPIs

---

### 2. Profitability & Cost Optimization
Examines profit drivers across products, regions, and discount levels to identify loss-making areas and opportunities for margin improvement.

**Key Focus:** Profitability analysis, pricing strategy, cost optimization

---

### 3. Sales Performance & Growth Trends
Tracks revenue, profit, and order growth over time to evaluate business performance and identify long-term trends.

**Key Focus:** Time-series analysis, growth metrics, trend evaluation

---

### 4. Zomato Data Analysis
Explores restaurant order data to understand customer behavior, revenue contribution, and operational outcomes.

**Key Focus:** Customer behavior analysis, sales performance, order analytics
