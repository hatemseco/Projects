# Zomato Data Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes Zomato restaurant order data to understand customer ordering behavior, revenue contribution, and operational performance. The goal is to identify top-performing food items, preferred payment methods, and delivery outcomes.

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Quantity Sold
- Successful vs Cancelled Orders

## Key Insights
- A small number of food items contribute a significant share of total revenue, indicating clear best-sellers.
- Online payment methods dominate transactions, reflecting strong customer preference for digital payments.
- Most orders are successfully delivered, suggesting stable operational performance.
- Certain items have high order volume but lower revenue contribution, highlighting pricing optimization opportunities.
