# Zomato Dataset Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This Power BI project analyzes a Zomato food delivery dataset to uncover insights related to revenue generation, customer ordering behavior, delivery performance, and payment method preferences.  
The dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders understand what drives sales, which food items and restaurants perform best, and where operational improvements can be made.

---

## Business Objectives
- Identify top-performing food items and restaurants
- Analyze total revenue and quantity sold
- Understand customer payment preferences
- Monitor delivery status efficiency
- Support data-driven decisions for menu optimization and operations

---

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **Total Revenue** – Overall sales generated from orders
- **Total Quantity Sold** – Total number of food items sold
- **Revenue by Food Item** – Identifies best-selling menu items
- **Revenue by Hotel** – Measures restaurant-level performance
- **Delivery Status Counts** – Delivered vs Cancelled vs In Progress
- **Payment Method Usage** – Card, UPI, Cash on Delivery (COD)

---

## Dashboard Insights
- **Card payments** are the most commonly used payment method, indicating a strong preference for digital transactions.
- **Chicken Biryani** is the top revenue-generating food item, followed by Cheeseburger variants.
- **Spice Paradise** contributes the highest share of total revenue among restaurants.
- The majority of orders are successfully **delivered**, with significantly fewer cancellations.
- Revenue is concentrated among a small number of popular items, suggesting opportunities for focused promotions.

---

## Visualizations Included
- KPI Cards for Revenue and Quantity
- Bar charts for revenue by food item
- Pie chart for hotel-wise revenue contribution
- Bar chart for delivery status distribution
- Slicers for payment method filtering

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI Desktop**

## Business Value
This dashboard enables food delivery businesses to:
- Optimize menu offerings
- Improve delivery efficiency
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing items
